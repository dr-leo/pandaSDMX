SDMX Information Model
======================

:mod:`pandasdmx.model` implements the SDMX Information Model (SDMX-IM). The
specification of the information model is available from the SDMX website:

- https://sdmx.org/?page_id=5008
- https://sdmx.org/wp-content/uploads/SDMX_2-1-1_SECTION_2_InformationModel_201108.pdf
